This window is used for issuing a book from the library.

Fill the Lib id in the given text field.

Fill the library card at the end of the book and submit to librarian.

Any student may only issue two books at a time.

The book must be returned with 14 days from the date of issue,
otherwise a fine of Rs 1/day is imposed.

Terms of issue may be changed without prior notice.

In case of any problems, contact the librarian.
